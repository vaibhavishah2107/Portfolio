Features

Six core features designed for students' mental wellness.

1. AI Companion

Conversational agent powered by Transformers for empathetic conversations, CBT-style prompts, and escalation to counselors when needed.

2. Gamified Engagement

Streaks, micro-challenges and rewards to encourage healthy habits and continuous use.

3. Screen Time & Attention Insights

Analytics showing most-used apps, focus interruptions, and suggestions to improve concentration.

4. Mood Tracker & Journaling

Quick mood check-ins, expressive journaling with automatic sentiment detection and trends over time.

5. Counselor Support & Booking

In-app scheduling, secure messaging, and escalation workflows compliant with privacy standards.

6. Research Dashboard & Insights

Aggregate anonymized metrics to inform campus interventions and program evaluation.
